## Design Notes
- Palette: cinnabar/vermillion red (from the clinic's own logo) as primary accent, warm near-black ink for text, a warm parchment/paper cream background, and a muted jade-green secondary accent derived from the red via `color-mix()` for a TCM-apothecary feel distinct from generic wellness-spa purple/teal. Serif display type (calligraphic/apothecary feel) paired with a clean sans body.
- Image layout pattern: aspect-ratio wrapper + `object-fit: cover` for all portrait/photo placements, for consistent cropping across breakpoints.
- Risk notes: only 3 authentic photos exist (2 similar practitioner portraits + 1 meditation photo) — deliberately used once each, on different pages/sections, not repeated, so the small asset pool doesn't read as repetitive. Genuinely multi-location business — scope is explicitly the London/Belsize Park clinic in title and hero copy per AGENTS.md; Welwyn Garden City clinic is honestly noted (not hidden) on the Contact page with its own address/hours, not built out as a full section.
